About Smart Livestock

Our mission is to empower the agricultural community with cutting-edge AI technology for enhanced livestock management and sustainable farming practices.

Our Vision

We envision a future where technology seamlessly integrates with traditional farming, providing actionable insights that lead to healthier animals, increased productivity, and more efficient resource utilization. Smart Livestock aims to be at the forefront of this transformation.

This project is proudly developed for the **Smart India Hackathon 2025**, showcasing innovative solutions for real-world challenges in agriculture.

Our Technology

At the core of Smart Livestock is a powerful **EfficientNet-B3** deep learning model, pre-trained on a vast dataset and fine-tuned on specific cattle breeds. This allows for highly accurate and fast recognition. Our web platform is built with **Flask**, ensuring a robust, scalable, and secure environment, while **modern JavaScript and CSS** provide an interactive and beautiful user experience.

We leverage technologies like TensorFlow, OpenCV, and modern web standards to deliver a seamless and intelligent application.
